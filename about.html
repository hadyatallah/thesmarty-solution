About The Smarty Solution Ltd

The Smarty Solution Ltd is a Cyprus-based company offering innovative solutions in the real estate sector, focusing on strategic land acquisition, development partnerships, and premium apartment construction. We specialize in helping individuals and investors access the unique opportunities Cyprus offers through legal residency, financial integration, and property ownership.

Our services include end-to-end assistance for opening bank accounts in Cyprus — a valuable step for international clients looking to benefit from Cyprus’s secure and business-friendly banking system. Cyprus is a member of the EU, with a favorable tax regime, low corporate tax (12.5%), and full access to European financial markets.

We also guide our clients through the process of acquiring permanent residency by investment on the island. This includes preparing and reviewing all necessary documentation, ensuring legal compliance, and offering personalized support throughout the process.

As part of our core business, we invest in plots of land and engage in joint ventures with reputable developers to build high-specification residential projects that match international quality standards. Our goal is to create lasting value for our partners and provide superior living spaces to future homeowners.
