Why Cyprus?

Cyprus, a beautiful Mediterranean island, offers more than just sunny beaches and scenic views. It's a growing business and lifestyle hub for entrepreneurs and investors.

  • Low corporate tax — just 12.5%, among the lowest in Europe
  • Residency by investment programs available to non-EU nationals
  • Upcoming inclusion in the Schengen Zone enhances travel and business across Europe
  • Stable legal system based on English Common Law
  • Safe, family-friendly environment with excellent private healthcare and education
